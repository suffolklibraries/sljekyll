In the lead-up to [Suffolk Libraries Day](/suffolk-libraries-day/) (Saturday 12 October), we’ve challenged ourselves to send a book by a local author to all 44 of our libraries, from Bungay to Haverhill.

Can your organisation facilitate and sponsor a leg of the book’s journey? Contact **nikki.hulse@suffolklibraries.co.uk** for more information and to get involved.
