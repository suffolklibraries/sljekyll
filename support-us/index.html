Support us

We're an independent, charitable organisation which has kept all Suffolk’s 44 libraries open, even though our budget has been reduced by a third since 2012. Help us carry on making life in Suffolk better.

Donate

You can donate online or by sending a text message. If you’re representing an organisation that would like to support us, contact our business development manager nikki.hulse@suffolklibraries.co.uk
