Support us

More than 3,800 people visit our libraries every week. We tackle social isolation, improve wellbeing and inform & entertain. Help us continue to make life better for the people of Suffolk.

Why we need your support

We’re a charity that provides all of Suffolk’s library services. Many people don’t realise we’re independent from Suffolk County Council.

Suffolk County Council provides most of our funding. Our budget is more than a third less than in 2011, and the Council continues to face cuts from central government.

We haven’t closed any libraries since taking over their control in 2011. We now reach thousands more people every week, through events, pre-school activities, wellbeing drop-ins and by simply providing a safe, non-judgemental place to be. And we lend physical and digital books.

We make life in Suffolk better and you can help us carry on our work by donating.
