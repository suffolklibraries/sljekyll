Support us

We're an independent, charitable organisation which has kept all 44 of Suffolk’s libraries open since taking them over in 2012. Help us continue to make life better for the people of Suffolk.

Donate

You can donate online or by sending a text message. If you’re representing an organisation that would like to support us, contact our business development manager nikki.hulse@suffolklibraries.co.uk
