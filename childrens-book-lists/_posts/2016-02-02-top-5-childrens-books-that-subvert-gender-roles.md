## [<cite>Bill’s new frock</cite> by Anne Fine](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ/16364299?QRY=CTIBIB%3C%20IRN(306175)&QRYTEXT=Bill%27s%20new%20frock)

<div class="row">
  <div class="medium-2 columns">
    <a href="https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ/16364299?QRY=CTIBIB%3C%20IRN(306175)&QRYTEXT=Bill%27s%20new%20frock" rel="attachment wp-att-54056"><img src="http://suffolklibraries.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/bills-new-frock.jpg" alt="Bill’s new frock by Anne Fine" class="aligncenter" /></a>
  </div>

  <div class="medium-10 columns">
    <p>
      The now classic story about Bill, a schoolboy who wakes up one morning to find he is a girl. That day at school, he learns a lot about gender stereotyping! And his frilly pink dress gets ruined.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="row">
  <div class="medium-2 columns">
    <a href="https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ/16365851?QRY=CTIBIB%3C%20IRN(41466)&QRYTEXT=The%20turbulent%20term%20of%20Tyke%20Tiler" rel="attachment wp-att-54059"><img src="http://suffolklibraries.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/the-turbulent-term-of-tyke-tiler.jpg" alt="The Turbulent term of Tyke Tiler by Gene Kemp" class="aligncenter" /></a>

</div>

## [<cite>Pippi Longstocking</cite> by Astrid Lindgren](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ/16366446?QRY=CTIBIB%3C%20IRN(50629)&QRYTEXT=Pippi%20Longstocking)

<div class="row">
  <div class="medium-2 columns">
    <a href="https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ/16366446?QRY=CTIBIB%3C%20IRN(50629)&QRYTEXT=Pippi%20Longstocking" rel="attachment wp-att-53496"><img src="http://suffolklibraries.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/pippi-longstocking.jpg" alt="Pippi Longstocking by Astrid Lindgren" class="aligncenter" /></a>
  </div>

  <div class="medium-10 columns">
    <p>
      If you want a role model that defies traditional gender roles, then meet Pippi, a 9-year-old girl with super strength. She stars in several of the books we have in stock.

  </div>
</div>

  <div class="medium-2 columns">
