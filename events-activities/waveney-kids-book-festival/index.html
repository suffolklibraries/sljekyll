Waveney Kids' Book Festival

Southwold and Beccles libraries, Southwold Arts Centre and Southwold Books 19 - 27 October 2019

A variety of events and activities based around books, writing and drawing for children of all ages this October half-term. Confirmed authors and illustrators so far include Kevin Crossley-Holland, Polly Dunbar, A. L. Kennedy, James Mayhew & Zeb Soanes, Julia Jones, Lisa Thompson, Francesca Armour-Chelu, Sophie Green and Linda Davies.
