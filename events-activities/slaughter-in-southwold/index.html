Slaughter in Southwold

Saturday 16 and Sunday 17 June 2018, Southwold Arts Centre

Join us for a feast of author talks, activities, food and film screenings. Tickets on sale from Southwold Library and Ticketsource from 1 April. £5 per author talk, £15 for a day pass, or £30 for the weekend.
