Slaughter in Southwold

The Friends of Southwold Library and the Crime Writers' Association present the third annual Slaughter in Southwold crime writers' festival at Southwold Arts Centre on Saturday 17 and Sunday 18 June.

You can buy tickets to individual events or our great value Saturday passes and Sunday passes and weekend passes Tickets and passes are also available at Southwold Library or on 01502 722519.

For further information please email charlotte.clark@suffolklibraries.co.uk
