Slaughter in Southwold

Saturday 17 and Sunday 18 June 2017

Join us for author talks, food and a quiz at the fifth annual Slaughter in Southwold crime writers' festival.

You can also win prizes in the Slaughter in Southwold raffle and by solving the Slaughter in Southwold 'Whodunnit' puzzle sheet.

Tickets and passes are on sale from 1 April.

Buy tickets for individual events or take advantage of our great value Saturday passes, Sunday passes and weekend passes.

Tickets and passes are also available at Southwold Library or on 01502 722519.
