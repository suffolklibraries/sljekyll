Thank you to all the children and volunteers who made this year's Summer Reading Challenge another success. Each of our libraries holds a presentation ceremony where children who completed the challenge can receive their certificates and medals.

Glemsford Library will be holding their presentation at Glemsford Primary Academy. Contact Glemsford Library if your child is not a pupil at the school.
