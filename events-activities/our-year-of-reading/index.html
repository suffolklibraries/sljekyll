What's on

Suffolk Writes

A pilot project to enable aspiring writers to get some of their work published as a library eBook which people can download from the library service.

Brave New Reads

A regional programme focusing on a list of six chosen books.

Summer Reading Challenge: The Big Friendly Read

The country’s biggest literacy programme aimed at children, based in libraries and growing in popularity every year. This is a great opportunity to encourage children to carry on reading through the holidays. Our libraries are looking for volunteers to help!
