As part of this year's Summer Reading Challenge we're running a gold ticket prize draw. Tickets are only £1 and can be bought in any of the county’s 44 libraries. The closing date for entries is 31 August.

‘Family prizes’ include free tickets for a family of 2 adults and 2 children, or 1 adult and 3 children.

See [full terms and conditions](/events-activities/summer-reading-challenge/gold-ticket-family-prize-draw/gold-ticket-prize-draw-terms-and-conditions).

## Prizes

Prizes are for a family of four unless otherwise stated (a family of four consisting of either: 2 adults and 2 children or 1 adult and 3 children)

### Prizes available in all libraries:

* Charlie and the Chocolate Factory Family West End Show
* Galloway Family Coach excursion to Cadbury World
* Bewilderwood Family Forest Adventure
* Roald Dahl Museum and Story Centre Family Pass
* Charlie and the Chocolate Factory book signed by Quentin Blake

* Sleeping Beauty Panto for the family at Ipswich Regent
* All Fired Up Ceramic painting party
* Namco family bowling experience
* Hollow Trees farm trail and roast dinner
* Museum of East Anglian Life family pass
* Easton Farm Park family tickets
* Kingpin family bowling experience
* Crown pools family swim
* Marina Lowestoft family cinema tickets
* Kingfisher leisure centre family swim
* Ipswich Transport Museum family tickets

### Non-family prizes:

* Colchester zoo (2 children’s tickets only)
* Chiquito £25 voucher
* Scribbles studio £10 voucher
