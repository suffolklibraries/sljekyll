Skulduggery in Stowmarket

27 - 30 April 2018

Join us for Suffolk's newest crime writers' festival. Enjoy talks from Nicola Upson, Alison Bruce, Jacqueline Beard, Edmund Wilson, Pauline Manders, Mandy Morton and more. There will also be a quiz, a murder mystery, workshops and activities for younger readers.

Watch this space or follow us on Facebook or Twitter for updates.
