Business advice

We have two New Anglia Growth Hub advisors working on a fortnightly, rotating basis. They advise on topics including exporting, digital support, intellectual property, finance, grants and vouchers, and starting up a business.

Kurt Scheepers

Kurt is a business growth adviser for south-east Suffolk and specialises in grant funding and business management.

MakerSpace

Situated in the Lab, our MakerSpace is an open, friendly public space to design and create. We offer a range of equipment including two 3D printers, a 3D scanner, two computer coding kits and two professional sewing machines. Find out about our facilities in the MakerSpace. Are you experienced with fashion and textile design, coding or 3D printers? We’re looking for skilled and enthusiastic volunteers to help support our newly built MakerSpace.
