<div class="row">
  <div class="medium-2 columns">
    <a href="https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ/23938324?QRY=CTIBIB%3C%20IRN(56734006)&QRYTEXT=Night%20thoughts%20%5Bsound%20recording%5D" rel="attachment wp-att-52570"><img class="alignnone" src="http://suffolklibraries.co.uk/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/night-thoughts.jpg" alt="Night thoughts by Suede" /></a>

  <div class="medium-10 columns">
    <p>
      Britpop veterans Suede return with a new album that they recently played to great acclaim at the Roundhouse. In the gig, Suede projected a film directed by Roger Sargent and written by Stephanie de Giorgio, with each narrative element operating as an enclosed promo to a particular song. Dark but fascinating.
