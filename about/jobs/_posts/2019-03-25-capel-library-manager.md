We're looking for a library manager at Capel St Mary Library. We're looking for an individual who can:

- Lead, manage and motivate a team of staff and volunteers to provide a customer focussed responsive service
- Engage and interact with people of all ages, build relationships and continue to develop the support from the local community
- Be proactive and promote innovative ideas that will help make the library relevant and increase use of the library service
- Support, assist and train staff and customers to access IT, based on personal knowledge of PCs, the internet, online databases, library computer systems and MS Office
- Work with the friends group to further benefit the residents of Capel and the local community

This role is for 15 hours 42 minutes on average per week in the following pattern:

**Week 1:** Tue 8:45am - 12:50pm; Wed 8:45am - 12:45pm; Thu 8:45am - 12:45pm; Fri 5pm - 7:45pm ; Sat 8:30am - 1pm

**Week 2:** Tue 8:45am - 12:50pm; Wed 8:45am - 12:45pm; Thu 8:45am - 12:45pm

The rate of pay is &pound;10.62 per hour (£20,495.69 pro-rata).

If you are interested, please send a CV and supporting statement outlining what you could bring to this position and Suffolk Libraries to: **recruitment@suffolklibraries.co.uk**

As users of the disability conﬁdent scheme, we guarantee to interview all disabled applicants who meet the minimum criteria for the vacancy.

The closing date for applications is **Monday 15 April 2019**. Interviews will be held on **Friday 26 April 2019**.
