Our mobile library service is a public access mobile library, visiting rural communities throughout Suffolk. We currently operate 3 large vehicles, visiting 587 different locations on a four weekly timetable.

We are currently looking for a mobile driver to drive a mobile library vehicle from our Saxmundham base across locations in Suffolk to an agreed schedule. We're looking for an individual who:

- Has extensive experience of driving (preferably a 7.5 tonne vehicle) and holds a full clean C1 category driving licence

- Can demonstrate appropriate and effective communication skills with the aim of building positive and supportive relations with our customers and community

- Is confident and competent in using the internet, email and a variety of devices and applications

- Can be proactive and promote innovative ideas that will help make the library relevant and increase the use of the mobile library service.

If you have experience in a customer facing role, enjoy helping people, hold a clean full C1 driving licence and an interest in books and computers, we would like to hear from you.

This position is for 10.63 hours on average per week working the following pattern:

**Week 1:** Fri 8.5 hours; Sat 8.5 hours

**Week 2:** Fri 8.5 hours

**Week 3:** Fri 8.5 hours

**Week 4:** Fri 8.5 hours

The rate of pay commences at &pound;9.96 per hour.

If you are interested, please send a CV and supporting statement outlining what you could bring to this position and Suffolk Libraries to: **recruitment@suffolklibraries.co.uk**

The closing date for applications is **Friday 2 February 2018**. Interviews will be held in the **week commencing 12 February 2018**.
