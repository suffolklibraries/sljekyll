We're looking for a manager at Aldeburgh Library. We're looking for a dynamic and forward thinking individual to:

- Lead, manage and motivate a team of staff and volunteers to provide a customer focussed responsive service

- Engage with the community, schools, and business partners to build relationships and continue to develop the support from the local community

- Be proactive and promote innovative ideas that will help make the library relevant and increase the use of the library service

- Spot new opportunities to link the organisation's activities to new funding sources

- Support, assist and train staff and customers to access IT, based on personal knowledge of PCs, the internet, online databases, library computer systems and MS Office

- Work with the Aldeburgh Library Foundation to further benefit the residents of Aldeburgh and the local community

This position is for 22.25 hours on average per week in the following work pattern:

Monday 7.25 hours - Wednesday 7.5 hours - Thursday 7.5 hours

The rate of pay is &pound;19,504.76 pro-rata - &pound;10.11 per hour.

If you are interested, please send a CV and supporting statement outlining what you could bring to this position and Suffolk Libraries to: **recruitment@suffolklibraries.co.uk**

The closing date for applications is **Sunday 19 March 2017**. Interviews will be held on **Thursday 30 March 2017 at Woodbridge Library**.
