We're looking for a manager at Long Melford Library. We're looking for a dynamic and forward thinking individual to:

- Lead, manage and motivate a team of staff and volunteers to provide a customer focussed responsive service

- Engage and interact with people of all ages, build relationships and continue to develop the support from the local community

- Be proactive and promote innovative ideas that will help make the library relevant and increase the use of the library service

- Support, assist and train staff and customers to access IT, based on personal knowledge of PCs, the internet, online databases, library computer systems and MS Office

- Work with the friends group to further benefit the residents of Long Melford and the local community.

This position is for 11.5 hours on average per week in the following pattern:

Tue 12pm - 3:30pm; Wed 2pm - 5pm; Fri 2pm - 7pm

The rate of pay is &pound;19,699.81 pro-rata &#8212; &pound;10.21 per hour.

If you are interested, please send a CV and supporting statement outlining what you could bring to this position and Suffolk Libraries to: **recruitment@suffolklibraries.co.uk**

The closing date for applications is **Sunday 4 March 2018**. Interviews will be held in the **week commencing 12 March 2018**.
