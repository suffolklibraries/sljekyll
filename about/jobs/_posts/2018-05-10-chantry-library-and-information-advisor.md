We're looking for two library and information advisors at Chantry Library. We're looking for individuals who:

- Have an aspiration to deliver excellent customer service

- Are calm under pressure and able to prioritise workload

- Have the ability to interact with all age groups

- Have interpersonal skills and the enthusiasm to build positive and supportive relations with our clients, team, partners and community

- Have a good level of writing, reading and numeracy skills

- Are confident and competent in using the internet and email and using a variety of devices and applications

- Have excellent digital skills

- Understand Suffolk Libraries' services and what it delivers

- Have the ability to organise, promote and run activities for children and adults.

If you have experience in a customer facing role, enjoy helping people, have a willingness to work within a team in a busy environment and an interest in books and computers, we would like to hear from you.

**Role 1** is a permanent position for 9 hours a week in the following pattern:

* Thurs 10am - 1pm; Sun 10am - 4pm

**Role 2** is a six-month fixed term contract for 6 hours per week in the following pattern:

* Sun 10am - 4pm

The rate of pay is &pound;8.17 per hour.

If you are interested, please send a CV and supporting statement outlining what you could bring to this position and Suffolk Libraries to: **recruitment@suffolklibraries.co.uk**

When applying, **please state which role you would like to apply for, or your order of preference if applying for either role**.

The closing date for applications is **Friday 1 June 2018**. Interviews will be held on **Friday 15 June 2018**.
