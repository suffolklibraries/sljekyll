We're looking for a HR administrator based at Ipswich County Library. The role involves:

- General HR administration, including assisting with general emails and telephone enquiries
- Ensuring that all HR and employee documentation is up-to-date and compliant
- Collating new starter information, including ID, eligibility paperwork and signed contracts, and ensuring all employee information is received in a timely manner
- Applying for and verifying DBS applications and Right to Work requirements.

We're looking for an individual who:

- Has previous administration/office experience
- Is highly organised, fast paced and able to use initiative
- Has a proactive and methodical approach to work, with a 'can do' attitude
- Can build effective relationships with line managers and employees at all levels
- Has good written and verbal communication skills.

This role is for 8 hours a week, either working one full day or split between two days.

The rate of pay is &pound;17,363 pro rata.

If you are interested, please send a CV and supporting statement outlining what you could bring to this position and Suffolk Libraries to: **recruitment@suffolklibraries.co.uk**

As users of the disability confident scheme, we guarantee to interview all disabled applicants who meet the minimum criteria for the vacancy.

The closing date for applications is **Tuesday 20 August 2019**. Interviews will be held on **Tuesday 27 August 2019**.

If you are invited to an interview, you will need to bring along some [documents to verify your identity](/about/jobs/verification-of-identity/).
