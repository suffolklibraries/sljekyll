We're looking for a library and information advisor to provide a high quality library service to prisoners at HMP Highpoint. We're looking for an individual who:

- Aspires to deliver great customer service
- Is friendly and approachable with a confident manner and the ability to remain vigilant
- Can demonstrate appropriate interpersonal and effective communication skills
- Enjoys reading and promoting reading to others and is willing to support and deliver the additional activities we offer
- Is IT literate and competent in the use of computer packages to provide information to customers
- Can demonstrate excellent problem solving skills and work on their own initiative as well as being an effective team member
- Has sufficient emotional resilience to work in what can be both a challenging and highly rewarding environment
- Understands Suffolk Libraries' services and what it delivers.

If you have experience in a customer facing role, enjoy helping people and have a willingness to work within a team in a busy environment, we would like to hear from you.

This role is for an average of 26.25 hours a week in the following pattern:

Mon 8am - 3:30pm; Tue 1pm - 7:30pm; Wed 7:45am - 3:30pm; Thu 1pm - 7:30pm

The rate of pay is &pound;8.17 per hour.

If you are interested, please send a CV and supporting statement outlining what you could bring to this position and Suffolk Libraries to: **recruitment@suffolklibraries.co.uk**

The closing date for applications is **Monday 22 October 2018**. Interviews will be held on **Monday 5 November 2018**.
