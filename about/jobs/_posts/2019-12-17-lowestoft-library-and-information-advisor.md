We're looking for a library and information advisor for 12 months' maternity cover at Lowestoft Library. We're looking for an individual who:

- Has an aspiration to deliver excellent customer service

- Has appropriate and effective communication skills to build positive and supportive relations with our customers, team, partners and community

- Can engage confidently with young people and is enthusiastic, keen and willing to deliver the programme of activities we offer including children's activities and crafts

- Is IT literate and competent in the use of the internet, email, mobile devices and applications and could also offer guidance to customers

- Can work as an effective team member

- Understands Suffolk Libraries' services and what it delivers.

If you have experience in a customer facing role, enjoy helping people, have a willingness to work within a team in a busy environment and an interest in books and computers, we would like to hear from you.

This role is for an average of 33.13 hours a week in the following pattern:

**Week 1**: Mon 8:45am - 5pm; Tue 10am - 7pm; Fri 9:30am - 6pm; Sat 9am - 5pm; Sun 9:45am - 4pm

**Week 2:** Tue 10am - 7pm; Wed 9am - 6pm; Thur 8:45am - 5pm; Fri 9:30am - 6pm

The rate of pay is &pound;8.17 per hour.

If you are interested, please send a CV and supporting statement outlining what you could bring to this position and Suffolk Libraries to: **recruitment@suffolklibraries.co.uk**

The closing date for applications is **Monday 14 January 2019**. Interviews will be held on **Monday 28 January 2019**.
