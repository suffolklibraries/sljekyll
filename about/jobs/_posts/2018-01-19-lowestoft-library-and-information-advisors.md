We're looking for 5 library and information advisors at Lowestoft Library. We're looking for individuals who:

- Have an aspiration to deliver excellent customer service

- Have interpersonal skills and the enthusiasm to build positive and supportive relations with our clients, team, partners and community

- Have a good level of writing, reading and numeracy skills

- Are confident and competent in the use of the internet, email, mobile devices and applications

- Have excellent digital skills

- Understand Suffolk Libraries' services and what it delivers.

If you have experience in a customer facing role, enjoy helping people, have a willingness to work within a team in a busy environment and an interest in books and computers, we would like to hear from you.

**Role 1** is for on average 28 hours a week in the following pattern:

  * **Week 1:** Tue 10am - 7pm; Fri 10:30am - 6pm; Sat 1pm - 5pm; Sun 9:45am - 4pm
  * **Week 2:** Mon 8:45am - 1pm; Tue 8:45am - 5pm; Wed 8:45am - 1pm; Thu 10am - 7pm; Fri 9am - 6pm

**Role 2** is for on average 18 hours a week in the following pattern:

  * **Week 1:** Mon 9:30am - 2pm; Tue 9:30am - 2pm; Wed 9:30am - 2pm; Thu 9:30am - 2pm
  * **Week 2:** Mon 9:30am - 2pm; Tue 9:30am - 2pm; Wed 9:30am - 2pm; Sat 10:30am - 3:30pm

**Role 3** is for on average 22 hours a week in the following pattern:

  * **Week 1:** Tue 8:45am - 5pm; Thu 8:45am - 5pm; Fri 10:30am - 6pm
  * **Week 2:** Mon 8:45am - 5pm; Thu 8:45am - 1pm; Sat 8:45am - 3:30pm; Sun 9:45am - 4pm

**Role 4** is for on average 19.25 hours a week in the following pattern:

  * **Week 1:** Mon 8:45am - 1pm; Wed 9:30am - 6pm; Sat 8:45am - 1pm; Sun 10:30am - 2pm
  * **Week 2:** Mon 8:45am - 5pm; Tue 8:45am - 1pm; Thu 10:30am - 7pm

**Role 5** is for on average 17.63 hours a week in the following pattern:

  * **Week 1:** Tue 10:30am - 7pm; Sat 8:45am - 3:30pm; Sun 9:45 - 4pm
  * **Week 2:** Wed 8:45am - 1pm; Thu 10:30am - 7pm; Fri 9:30am - 2pm

The rate of pay is &pound;8.01 per hour.

If you are interested, please send a CV and supporting statement outlining what you could bring to this position and Suffolk Libraries to: **recruitment@suffolklibraries.co.uk**

The closing date for applications is **Friday 2 February 2018**. Interviews will be held in the **week commencing 19 February 2018**.
