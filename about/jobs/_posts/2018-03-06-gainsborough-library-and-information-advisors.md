We're looking for two library and information advisors at Gainsborough Community Library. We're looking for individuals who:

- Can engage confidently and effectively with large groups of young people

- Have an aspiration to deliver excellent customer service

- Can demonstrate appropriate and effective communication skills with the aim of building positive and supportive relations with our customers, team and community

- Are confident and competent in using the internet, email and using a variety of devices and applications

- Understand Suffolk Libraries' services and what it delivers

- Have a passion for community engagement.

If you have experience in a customer facing role, enjoy helping people, have a willingness to work within a team in a busy environment and an interest in books and computers, we would like to hear from you.

**Role 1** is for on average 27 hours a week in the following pattern:

  * **Week 1:** Mon 8:45am - 6:15pm; Fri 8:45am - 6:15pm
  * **Week 2:** Mon 8:45am - 6:15pm; Tue 8:45am - 5:15pm; Fri 8:45am - 6:15pm; Sat 8:45am - 5:15pm; Sun 11am - 4pm

**Role 2** is for on average 26.125 hours a week in the following pattern:

  * **Week 1:** Tue 8:45am - 5:15pm; Wed 8:45am - 5:15pm; Thu 11am - 5:15pm; Fri 1pm - 6:15pm; Sat 8:45am - 5:15pm
  * **Week 2:** Wed 8:45am - 5:15pm; Thu 11am - 5:15pm; Sun 10:45am - 4:15pm

The rate of pay is &pound;8.01 per hour.

If you are interested, please send a CV and supporting statement outlining what you could bring to this position and Suffolk Libraries to: **recruitment@suffolklibraries.co.uk**

When applying **please clearly state which role(s) you would like to be considered for**.

The closing date for applications is **Wednesday 21 March 2018**. Interviews will be held on **Thursday 5 April 2018**.
