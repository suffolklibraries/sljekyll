We're looking for a HR advisor based at Ipswich County Library to:

- Be a first line response for HR queries, including those to the HR inbox
- Ensure HR files and the HR system are kept up to date
- Compile and issue HR correspondence
- Support the recruitment process and ensure the appropriate paperwork is completed
- Onboard employees and workers, ensuring compliance with legislation, policy and process while maintaining excellent communication. This includes, but is not limited to: recruitment administration and authorisations, new starter paperwork collation, induction, HR system inputting and filing
- Assisting line managers on staffing issues such as absenteeism, sickness cover, disciplinary matters and the grievance procedure.

We're looking for an individual who:

- Has previous administration/office experience
- Can provide basic advice on employment terms, conditions, policies and procedures
- Is highly organised, fast paced and able to use initiative
- Has a proactive and methodical approach to work with a 'can do' attitude
- Can build effective relationships with line managers and employees at all levels.

This role is for three full days a week (22 hours).

The rate of pay is &pound;16,500-17,000 pro rata.

If you are interested, please send a CV and supporting statement outlining what you could bring to this position and Suffolk Libraries to: **recruitment@suffolklibraries.co.uk**

The closing date for applications is **Friday 22 June 2018**. Interviews will be held in the week commencing **25 June 2018**.
