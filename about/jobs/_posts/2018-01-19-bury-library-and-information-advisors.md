We're looking for 5 library and information advisors at Bury St Edmunds Library. We're looking for individuals who:

- Have an aspiration to deliver excellent customer service

- Have interpersonal skills and the enthusiasm to build positive and supportive relations with our clients, team, partners and community

- Have a good level of writing, reading and numeracy skills

- Are confident and competent in the use of the internet, email, mobile devices and applications

- Have excellent digital skills

- Understand Suffolk Libraries' services and what it delivers.

If you have experience in a customer facing role, enjoy helping people, have a willingness to work within a team in a busy environment and an interest in books and computers, we would like to hear from you.

**Role 1** is for on average 21.5 hours a week in the following pattern:

  * **Week 1:** Mon 11am - 4pm; Wed 1pm - 5:30pm; Fri 9am - 2pm; Sat 8:30am - 5pm
  * **Week 2:** Tue 8:30am - 5pm; Thu 8:30am - 5:30pm; Fri 2pm - 7:30pm

**Role 2** is for on average 23.25 hours a week in the following pattern:

  * **Week 1:** Wed 9am - 2pm; Thu 8:30am - 5:30pm; Fri 2pm - 7:30pm
  * **Week 2:** Mon 8:30am - 5:30pm; Tue 10am - 3pm; Thu 9am - 2pm; Sat 1pm - 5pm; Sun 9:45am - 4:15pm

**Role 3** is for on average 21.38 hours a week in the following pattern:

  * **Week 1:** Mon 10am - 3pm; Tue 10am - 3pm; Wed 8:30am - 5pm; Fri 8:30am - 4pm; Sat 1pm - 5pm
  * **Week 2:** Tue 3pm - 7:45pm; Wed 9am - 2pm; Fri 10am - 3pm

**Role 4** is for on average 22.88 hours a week in the following pattern:

  * **Week 1:** Tue 8:30am - 2pm; Thu 8:30am - 5:30pm; Fri 3pm - 7:45pm
  * **Week 2:** Mon 11am - 4pm; Wed 1pm - 5:30pm; Fri 9am - 2pm; Sat 8:30am - 5pm; Sun 10am - 4pm

**Role 5** is for on average 21.25 hours a week in the following pattern:

  * **Week 1:** Tue 8:30am - 4pm; Thu 8:30am - 5pm; Fri 10am - 3pm
  * **Week 2:** Mon 8:30am - 5pm; Tue 2pm - 7:30pm; Sat 10am - 3pm; Sun 10am - 4pm

The rate of pay is &pound;8.01 per hour.

If you are interested, please send a CV and supporting statement outlining what you could bring to this position and Suffolk Libraries to: **recruitment@suffolklibraries.co.uk**

The closing date for applications is **Friday 2 February 2018**. Interviews will be held in the **week commencing 19 February 2018**.
