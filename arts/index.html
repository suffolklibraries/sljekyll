Meet Melissa Matthews, our arts coordinator

Melissa joined us in April 2018 to oversee our ACE-funded young people's arts project. Since then, she has been working with staff and partner organisations to develop the programme.

The story so far

Over the past few months, we have:

  • Welcomed arts coordinator Melissa Matthews to oversee our countywide arts programme
  • Worked with partner organisations to develop the project. These include well-established national arts organisation METAL, who we will be collaborating with for the first year of the scheme.
  • Worked with young people and education professionals to develop the themes, activities and branding of the programme. Young people will continue to be involved with the project's evolution as the Suffolk Libraries Youth Arts Council.
  • Trained our staff to develop and deliver their ideas at an 'Arts Bootcamp'.

Culture Lab

We have recruited 10 artists from a variety of disciplines and backgrounds for a week-long residency at Ipswich County Library. During this pilot, from 30 July - 3 August, these artists will focus on engaging people aged 11-25 from all walks of life through exploration and experimentation with different media.

On Thursday 2 August at 7pm, we will be holding a public event at Ipswich County Library. We will be officially launching the arts programme, unveiling its name and branding and showcasing the work of the 10 artists in residence. Email Melissa at arts@suffolklibraries.co.uk if you would like to come.
