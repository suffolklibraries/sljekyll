[Reading Well](http://reading-well.org.uk/books/books-on-prescription/young-people-mental-health) have compiled a list of 35 recommended books on the topic of young people's mental health. You can borrow these books from our libraries or from our eLibrary service.

## General

* [<cite>Stuff That Sucks: Accepting What You Can’t Change and Committing to What You Can</cite> by Ben Sedley](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=1835954)
* [<cite>Mind Your Head</cite> by Juno Dawson](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=1896983)
* [<cite>The Self-Esteem Team’s Guide to Sex, Drugs and WTFs?!!</cite> by The Self-Esteem Team](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=1843507)
* [<cite>Blame My Brain: The Amazing Teenage Brain Revealed</cite> by Nicola Morgan](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=1370342)
* [<cite>Quiet the Mind</cite> by Matthew Johnstone](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=554993)
* [<cite>I’ll Give You the Sun</cite> by Jandy Nelson](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=1737136)
* [<cite>Kite Spirit</cite> by Sita Brahmachari](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=1369546)
* [<cite>House of Windows</cite> by Alexia Casale](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=1789054)
* [<cite>Every Day</cite> by David Levithan](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=1395891)
