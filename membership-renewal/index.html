Suffolk Libraries Extra is a supporter scheme offering individuals and local businesses the chance to make an annual donation to get more involved and enhance the service Suffolk Libraries provides.

You can support Suffolk Libraries by signing up at one of three price levels:

  • Silver - £20 per year
  • Gold - £50 per year
  • Platinum - £150 per year

If you'd like to help us by donating a different amount, please call us on 01473 351249.

Sign up

You can sign up online or pay in any library or by calling 01473 351249.

We use PayPal to securely process payments online. You do not need a PayPal account to join Suffolk Libraries Extra.

How your donation will help us

Donations will go into a dedicated Development Fund which will be used to support and improve the work of our 44 libraries in their local communities.

The scheme is completely voluntary. At the end of the year we’ll ask if you’d like to sign up again.

Read about some of the projects the Suffolk Libraries Extra scheme has supported so far →

What you'll get in return

All members of Suffolk Libraries Extra will get a membership card and a special newsletter with exclusive recommendations, advance notice of new library titles and exclusive offers and competitions. So far we've given away signed books, an annual family membership to English Heritage and a year's subscription to Suffolk Magazine.

Platinum level members will also have the option of attending our AGM and be acknowledged in our Annual Review next year.

Suffolk Libraries Extra aims to enhance the library service by funding improvements while continuing to procide the same free services and benefits.

If you’d like to know more or would like help with your application, email extra@suffolklibraries.co.uk
