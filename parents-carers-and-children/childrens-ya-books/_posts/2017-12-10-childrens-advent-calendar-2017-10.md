Every day until Christmas Eve, stock librarian Sophie will recommend one of our brilliant new Christmas books for children. Today's book is [<cite>Nadiya's Bake Me a Festive Story: thirty festive recipes and stories for children</cite>, by Nadiya Hussain & Clair Rossiter](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2257531).

![Nadiya's Bake Me a Festive Story](/images/featured/featured-nadiyas-bake-me-a-festive-story.jpg)

> "Join Great British Bake Off sensation Nadiya Hussain for scrumptious stories and delicious bakes that the family can enjoy together at Christmas.

> "Christmas is a time when families come together. This unique combination of storybook and cookbook, with all recipes and stories devised and written by Nadiya herself, brings families into the kitchen to share this special time of year through cooking and stories."
