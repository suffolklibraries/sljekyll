Every day until Christmas Eve, stock librarian Sophie will recommend one of our brilliant new Christmas books for children. Today's book is [<cite>The Princess and the Christmas Rescue</cite>, by Caryl Hart & Sarah Warburton](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2255616).

![The Princess and the Christmas Rescue](/images/featured/featured-princess-and-the-christmas-rescue.jpg)

> "Princess Eliza is awfully lonely in her beautiful snow palace. Stuck inside all day with no-one to play with, she's become brilliant at building her own toys. Eliza can make anything with a few bits of wood and some string! Anything, that is, except the thing she wants most: a friend!"
