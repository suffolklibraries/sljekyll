Every day until Christmas Eve, stock librarian Sophie will recommend one of our brilliant new Christmas books for children. Today's book is [<cite>Snow in the Garden: a first book of Christmas</cite>, by Shirley Hughes](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2444982).

> "This beautiful Christmas anthology contains winter adventures, seasonal poems, festive recipes and easy-to-make craft activities, as well as Shirley Hughes' trademark warm and classic illustrations.

> "Whether reading stories and poems to little ones or encouraging them to make beautiful crafts and Christmas treats, this title is the perfect introduction to this very special time of year."
