Every day until Christmas Eve, stock librarian Sophie will recommend one of our brilliant new Christmas books for children. Today's book is [<cite>Christmas comes to Moominvalley</cite>, by Alex Haridi, Cecilia Davidsson, Tove Jansson & Filippa Widlund](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2459065).

![Christmas comes to Moominvalley](/images/featured/featured-christmas-comes-to-moominvalley.jpg)

> "An exquisitely illustrated re-telling of Tove Jansson's classic story <cite>The Fir Tree</cite>, in which the Moomins are woken up from their winter sleep to be told that 'Christmas' is coming. The only trouble is, the Moomins have no idea who, or what, Christmas is."
