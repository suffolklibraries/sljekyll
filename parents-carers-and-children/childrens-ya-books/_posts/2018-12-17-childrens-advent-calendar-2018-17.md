Every day until Christmas Eve, stock librarian Sophie will recommend one of our brilliant new Christmas books for children. Today's book is [<cite>Dinosaurs Go Christmas Shopping</cite>, by Timothy Knapman & Sarah Warburton](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2450613).

![Dinosaurs Go Christmas Shopping](/images/featured/featured-dinosaurs-go-christmas-shopping.jpg)

> "When a group of unruly dinosaurs threaten to ruin Christmas, one little boy decides to take charge. Will the dinosaurs listen? You bet they will! And after a lot of sorting, cleaning and tidying, it's very clear that this will definitely be the best Christmas ever. Hooray for the dinosaurs!"
