If you're feeling lonely or isolated, Elmswell Library are offering extra support this Christmas.

That's not all that's happening this month. Five of our libraries are hosting Librarian Theatre's production, Alice in the Cuckoo's Nest, and a few libraries are hosting Suffolk Libraries Presents screenings. Haverhill Library will be holding a Harry Potter book trail around the town as our Harry Potter: A History of Magic display moves there on 8 December.
