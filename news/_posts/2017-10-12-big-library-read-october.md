From 12-26 October, you can borrow <cite>The Hundred Lies of Lizzie Lovett</cite> by Chelsea Sedoti on OverDrive without a wait list and join in a worldwide discussion.

<cite>The Hundred Lies of Lizzie Lovett</cite> won an online poll which invited borrowers to choose the next title for the Big Library Read, "the only global eBook reading club". As with physical library books, OverDrive titles' availability is usually subject to demand from other users. By taking such restrictions off <cite>The Hundred Lies of Lizzie Lovett</cite> for two weeks, OverDrive have given readers at thousands of libraries the opportunity to borrow this book simultaneously, encouraging them to read and discuss the book.

You can borrow <cite>The Hundred Lies of Lizzie Lovett</cite>, find out more about the book and join the discussion from the [Suffolk Libraries OverDrive catalogue](https://suffolklibraries.overdrive.com/). For help setting up OverDrive, please see [our OverDrive instructions page](/elibrary/overdrive/).
