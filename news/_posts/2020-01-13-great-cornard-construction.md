The main entrance to [Great Cornard Library](/libraries/great-cornard-library/) is out of use until March for construction work to improve disabled access. The library is open as normal and customers can enter the library through Great Cornard Leisure Centre and Gainsborough Coffee House.

The work is being funded by housing developer contributions and Thomas Gainsborough School.
