We held the 3rd Suffolk Libraries Annual General Meeting last night at Suffolk Food Hall in Wherstead, Ipswich.

During the meeting [the society](http://suffolklibraries.co.uk/about):

  * accepted the previous financial year accounts
  * chose auditors for the next financial year
  * voted for two board member positions

Tony Brown and Jonathan Ogden were required to step down from the Board and stood for re-election with three new candidates. The society re-elected both. The board comprises:

  * Tony Brown (Beccles)
  * Sylvia Knights (Bungay)
  * Sue Buck (Long Melford)
  * Anne Mallalieu (Glemsford)
  * Jonathan Ogden (Kesgrave)
  * Colin Rockall (Lavenham)
  * Peter Callaghan (Brandon)

The vote for chair, vice-chair and honorary secretary will take place at the first board meeting after the AGM, on 24 September 2015.

* * *

Joanna Trollope gave a speech about the her role in the Library Task Force, noting how impressed she is with Suffolk Libraries and how the Suffolk Libraries model is a benchmark for other authorities. Her words were ‘Suffolk has got it!’
