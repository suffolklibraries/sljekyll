On Saturday 9 November, the Takeover Festival will bring workshops, talks, exhibitions and live music to Ipswich County Library. The festival is wholly organised by young people and facilitated by Suffolk Libraries' youth arts programme, [BLOC - Building Libraries on Creativity](/bloc/).
