Each month BBC Radio Suffolk presenter Lesley Dolphin is in conversation with journalist, Catherine Larner as part of the Lesley's Reads monthly book group.

This month the chosen book is **<cite>The Last Pilot</cite>** by Benjamin Johncock which is also one of this year's [Brave New Reads](/new-suggestions/fiction/brave-new-reads-2016) books.

You can get involved! If you've read the book you can join in the discussion on the programme at 2pm on Tuesday 14 June. If you’d like to join in, call Radio Suffolk on 01473 250000, tweet [@lesleydolphin](http://www.twitter.com/lesleydolphin) or visit [www.facebook.com/LesleysReaders](http://www.facebook.com/LesleysReaders).

You can also find out more about the book and borrow it from the [Suffolk Libraries catalogue](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?ENTRY_NAME=TI&amp;ENTRY=last+pilot&amp;ENTRY1_OPER=%2B&amp;ENTRY2_NAME=AU&amp;ENTRY2=&amp;ENTRY2_TYPE=K&amp;ENTRY3_NAME=SU&amp;ENTRY3=&amp;ENTRY_TYPE=K&amp;ENTRY3_OPER=%2B&amp;NRECS=20&amp;SORTS=HBT.SOVR&amp;SEARCH_FORM=%2Fcgi-bin%2Fspydus.exe%2FMSGTRN%2FOPAC%2FBSEARCH&amp;CF=GEN&amp;ISGLB=0&amp;GQ=last+pilot).
