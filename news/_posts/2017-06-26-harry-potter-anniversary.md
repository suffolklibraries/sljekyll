To celebrate the 20th anniversary of <cite>Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</cite>, from 26 June - 9 July, you can borrow it on OverDrive without a wait list.

As with physical library books, OverDrive titles' availability is usually subject to demand from other users. They have taken these restrictions off <cite>Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</cite> for two weeks, giving readers at thousands of libraries the opportunity to borrow it simultaneously and relive the magic or experience it for the first time. Customers can borrow the title for the standard loan period of three weeks.

To borrow <cite>Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone</cite>, visit the [OverDrive website](https://suffolklibraries.overdrive.com/media/790239). For help setting up OverDrive, please see [our OverDrive instructions page](/elibrary/overdrive/).
