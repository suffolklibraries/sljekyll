From 16-30 March, you can borrow [<cite>Art of the Pie</cite>](https://suffolklibraries.overdrive.com/media/2736161) by Kate McDermott on OverDrive without a wait list and join in a worldwide discussion.

OverDrive have chosen this book for their Big Library Read, which creates "the only global eBook reading club". As with physical library books, OverDrive titles' availability is usually subject to demand from other users. By taking such restrictions off <cite>Art of the Pie</cite> for two weeks, OverDrive have given readers at thousands of libraries the opportunity to borrow this book simultaneously, encouraging them to read and discuss the book.

You can borrow <cite>Art of the Pie</cite>, find out more about the book and join the discussion from the [Suffolk Libraries OverDrive catalogue](https://suffolklibraries.overdrive.com/). For help setting up OverDrive, please see [our OverDrive instructions page](/elibrary/overdrive/).
