Thurston Library is proposing to change its Tuesday opening hours. Currently, it’s open 3-7pm and is proposing to change to 2-6pm.

If you’d like to comment on this proposal please fill in a customer consultation form. Forms are available from [Thurston Library](/libraries/thurston-library/).
