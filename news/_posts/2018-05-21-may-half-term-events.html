Wondering what to do during half-term? We've got children's activities planned across our libraries.

As well as several Elmer Day events across the county, children can enjoy Beach Bonkers events at three libraries, craft sessions, a film screening, a magic show and an author visit.

All of our libraries will be closed on Monday 28 May due to the Bank Holiday.
