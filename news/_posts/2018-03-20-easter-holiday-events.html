Wondering what to do during the Easter holidays? We've got children's activities planned across our libraries.

Children can hunt for Easter eggs, make Easter-themed crafts and Lego structures, have beach-themed fun, learn how to code, watch films, hear stories and much more.

All of our libraries will be closed for Easter on Friday 30 March, Sunday 1 April and Monday 2 April.
