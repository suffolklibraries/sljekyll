Wondering what to do this half-term? We've got entertainment and activities for children across our libraries.

As it's Halloween the following week, we've naturally organised a lot of spooky craft sessions and activities. We've also got animal encounters, craft sessions, film screenings, Lego building and science experiences.

Half-term is going to be particularly lively at Lowestoft Library, where lots of Flipside Festival events for children are taking place from 24-26 October.

Older children might be interested in the Harry Potter: A History of Magic display, which is at Ipswich County Library from Friday 20 October, as well as our livestream of the British Library's Hogwarts Curriculum Lecture: Astronomy with Maggie Aderin-Pocock at Ipswich County Library on Sunday 29 October.
