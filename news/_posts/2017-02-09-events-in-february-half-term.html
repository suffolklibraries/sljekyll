Wondering what to do with your children this February half-term? We're holding events for children across our libraries, many with a magical theme.

Some Presents screenings are also taking place in half-term. Hadleigh Library are showing York Theatre Royal's production of The Railway Children on Wednesday 15 February, while Felixstowe Library's screening of Twelfth Night on Friday 17 February may be of interest to older children who are studying Shakespeare at school.
