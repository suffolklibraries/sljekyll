Suffolk Libraries and Suffolk County Council are working together on plans which would mean a brand new library in Eye.

Planning and development is set to progress throughout 2018, with the new library likely to open in spring 2019.

To find out more, visit [Suffolk County Council's website](https://www.suffolk.gov.uk/council-and-democracy/council-news/show/plans-for-a-new-library-in-eye-progressing-well).
