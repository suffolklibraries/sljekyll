Lowestoft Library is playing a big role in the new Flipside Lowestoft festival later this month.

The library is hosting a wide range of events during half-term between Tuesday 24 October and Thursday 26 October.

Flipside Lowestoft is aimed at children and young people aged up to 18. There’s a wealth of free activity each day from 10am–4pm at the library as well as the Marina Theatre, Market Place and London Road North. The festival promoses a vibrant and jam-packed three days of music, theatre, art, dance and literature.

The library events include author events, storytelling, a puppet show, workshops in creating superheroes, poetry and carnival headdresses and much more!

A list of Flipside events at the library follows. For a full list of Flipside Lowestoft activities or more information about the festival, visit the Flipside Festival website.
