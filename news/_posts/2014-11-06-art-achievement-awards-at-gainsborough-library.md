15 young people received Bronze Art Awards and 6 younger children received Art Achievement Awards at Gainsborough Library this week.

The Mayor of Ipswich, Bill Quinton, presented the awards which were achieved completing the Summer Art Project at the library which was funded by the Starbucks Youth Action Programme who contributed £2,000.
