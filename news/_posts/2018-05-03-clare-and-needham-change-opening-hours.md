Following a recent consultation with customers Clare Library and Needham Market Library are changing their opening hours from Monday 5 June.

The total number of opening hours will stay the same but both libraries are changing their hours with the aim of providing more consistent hours in line with the needs of local residents.

Local people were asked for their views on the proposals before the changes were approved recently by the Suffolk Libraries Board and Suffolk County Council.

**Clare Library's new opening hours from 5 June:**

* Monday - closed (no change)
* Tuesday - 10am - 1pm and 2pm - 5pm (no change)
* Wednesday - 10am - 1pm and 2pm - 5pm (no change)
* Thursday - closed (no change)
* Friday - 10am - 1pm and 2pm - 5pm (currently 12:30pm - 5pm)
* Saturday - 10am - 1pm (currently 9:30am - 1pm)
* Sunday - 10am - 2pm (currently 11am - 4pm)

**Needham Market Library's new opening hours from 5 June:**

* Monday - closed (no change)
* Tuesday - 10am - 1pm and 2pm - 5pm (currently 10am - 3pm)
* Wednesday - 2pm - 7pm (currently 2pm - 5pm and 5:30pm - 7:30pm)
* Thursday - 10am - 1pm and 2pm - 5pm (no change)
* Friday - 2pm - 5pm (no change)
* Saturday - 10am - 1pm and 2pm - 5pm (currently 10am - 5pm)
* Sunday - 11am - 4pm (no change)
