Wondering what to do with your children this May half-term? We've got a variety of events to entertain them.

We also have a couple of special events for all ages during the week. Broomhill Library are hosting an Arts La'Olam Story Seekers performance in Broomhill Park (weather permitting) on Monday 29 May. Southwold Library are screening York Theatre Royal's production of The Railway Children at Southwold Arts Centre on Thursday 1 June. There will be a matinee showing and an evening showing.
