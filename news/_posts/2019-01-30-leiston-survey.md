Leiston Library are asking local people how they use the library.

Staff at Leiston would like to know how residents use the library, and what services and activities they would like to see in the future. They would especially like to hear from people who do not currently use the library.

Customers can fill in the [Leiston Library community survey](https://www.surveymonkey.co.uk/r/V9WLRQV) online or in the library. The deadline for completing the survey is **Wednesday 27 February**.
