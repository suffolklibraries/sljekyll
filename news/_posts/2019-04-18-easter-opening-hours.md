All of our libraries will be closed on Good Friday, Easter Sunday and Easter Monday (19, 21 and 22 April). They will be open as usual on Saturday 20 April. [Find out your local library's opening hours &rarr;](/libraries/)

Don't forget that you can still reserve and renew books in [our online catalogue](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/MSGTRN/OPAC/BSEARCH?HOMEPRMS=GENPARAMS) and download eBooks, eAudiobooks and digital newspapers, magazines and music from [our eLibrary](/elibrary/) while our libraries are closed.

Happy Easter from everyone at Suffolk Libraries.
