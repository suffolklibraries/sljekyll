All of our libraries are closed on 24, 25 and 26 December 2018 and 1 January 2019. Check your local library's page for individual opening times.

Our libraries will be open as normal on Monday 31 December with the exception of Bury St Edmunds Library, Gainsborough Community Library, Ipswich County Library, Lowestoft Library, which will be closing at 5pm.

In the meantime, our libraries are holding a variety of seasonal events, including:

  • Christmas fairs
  • Visits from Father Christmas
  • Craft sessions for adults and children
  • Carol singing and parties
