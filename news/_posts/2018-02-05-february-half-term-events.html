Wondering what to do this half-term? We've got children's activities planned across our libraries.

Children are invited to make balloon models, lanterns, Lego structures Valentine's-themed crafts and more. They can hear expert storytellers spinning their yarns and make new scientific discoveries.

Our Harry Potter: A History of Magic display from the British Library will be at Newmarket Library from 8 - 28 February.

Older children may be interested in Bears & Vagabonds theatre company's production, Lear, Gainsborough Community Library on Thursday 15 February. Young music-lovers are very welcome at The Burkitt Family's evening concert at Woodbridge Library of Friday 16 February.
