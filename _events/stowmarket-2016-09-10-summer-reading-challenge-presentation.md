Congratulations to all of our young readers who completed The Summer Reading
Challenge, we will be celebrating your achievement in our Medals and Certificates
Presentation Ceremony. Bring along your family and friends, we hope to see you
there!

The Summer Reading Challenge Presentation's prize giving, followed by a bric-a-brac stall, produce table, cake sale and book sale.

County Councillor's Gary Green, Stephen Searle and the Major of Stowmarket
Councillor Barry Humphreys will be presenting the awards.
