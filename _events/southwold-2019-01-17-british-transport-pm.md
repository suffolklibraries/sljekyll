Join us for a collection of archive films produced by Creative Arts East as part of the BFI’s Britain on Film project, revealing hidden histories and forgotten stories of people and places from the UK’s film and TV archives. Clips include “Holiday (Blackpool Rock)”, “East Anglian Holiday”, “Cyclist Special” & “West Country Journey”.

The screening will be followed by an optional fish & chip lunch at 12:45pm.

Book online or contact the library.
