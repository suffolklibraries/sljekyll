Children are invited to watch <cite>Willy Wonka & the Chocolate Factory</cite> (1971), make Willy Wonka and Oompa Loompa masks and pipe cleaner lollipops, create and eat their own new chocolate bars and Skittle art, and try a fruity Oompa Loompa.

Under 8s must be accompanied by an adult.
