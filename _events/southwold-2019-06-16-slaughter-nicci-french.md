Nicci French - AKA Sean French and Nicci Gerrard - will be discussing their bestselling Frieda Klein series, which recently concluded with <cite>Day of the Dead</cite>.

<img src="/images/featured/featured-nicci-french-2.jpg" alt="Sean French and Nicci Gerrard" class="custom-br-50 mw-40 {% include /c/img-float-right.html %}" />

Nicci and Sean have written 21 bestselling psychological thrillers together, with their novels being available in 31 languages. Their next book, <cite>The Lying Room</cite>, comes out in October and is their first standalone novel in 10 years. They have four grown-up children and divide their time between London and Suffolk. You can find out more about them in our [Meet the Author interview](/new-suggestions/meet-the-author/meet-the-author-nicci-french/).
