Join celebrated cartoonist and author Gary Northfield in the children's section as he introduces Julius Zebra.

<cite>Madagascar</cite> meets <cite>Gladiator</cite> in this action-packed and hysterically funny draw-along event. Gary has also created comics for <cite>The Beano</cite>, <cite>The Phoenix</cite> and <cite>Horrible Histories</cite> magazines. Listen to a reading and learn all the tricks of the trade from a comic book creator.

Under 12s must be accompanied by an adult.
