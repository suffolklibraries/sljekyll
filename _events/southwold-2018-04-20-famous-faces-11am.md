This archive film produced by Creative Arts East reveals hidden histories and forgotten stories of people and places across Norfolk and Suffolk from the UK's film and TV archives.

<cite>Famous Faces in East Anglia</cite> includes Alfred Hitchcock on a visit to Cambridge, Muhammad Ali in Norwich for an Ovaltine sponsored promotional tour, George Bernard Shaw at home in Hertfordshire and George Formby at a motocross rally in Norfolk, as well as a documentary, <cite>Benjamin Britten & His Festival</cite>, featuring Peter Pears, Henry Moore and Imogen Holst, as well as the Queen opening Snape Maltings Concert Hall.

Optional lunch must be booked in advance.
