Nicci French - AKA Sean French and Nicci Gerrard - will be discussing how they work together on their bestselling novels.

<img src="/images/featured/featured-nicci-french-2.jpg" alt="Sean French and Nicci Gerrard" class="custom-br-50 mw-40 {% include /c/img-float-right.html %}" />

Nicci and Sean have written more than twenty psychological thrillers together. <cite>Day of the Dead</cite>, the final volume in the Frieda Klein series, has just been published in paperback. They have four grown-up children and divide their time between London and Suffolk.
