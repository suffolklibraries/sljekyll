Hear about Merry Owen and her discovery of a mysterious and dangerous buried chest, then make your own bow and arrow.

<img src="/images/featured/featured-linda-davies.jpg" alt="Linda Davies" class="custom-br-50 mw-40 {% include /c/img-float-right.html %}" />

Linda Davies grew up in the Welsh mountains and is herself an avid longbow girl and rider. She now lives in Suffolk with her husband and three children. [<cite>Longbow Girl</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=1793823) is her first children's book. She has also written thrillers for adults and a non-fiction book, [<cite>Hostage</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=1700392), about her experience of being kidnapped at sea by Iranian government forces.
