Mick Herron will be talking about his award-winning Slough House novels, featuring the inimitable Jackson Lamb.

<img src="/images/featured/featured-mick-herron-square.jpg" alt="Mick Herron" class="custom-br-50 mw-40 {% include /c/img-float-right.html %}" />

Mick  was born in Newcastle-upon-Tyne, and now lives in Oxford. His Slough House novels have been shortlisted for a variety of awards, with [<cite>Dead Lions</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2270016) winning the CWA Goldsboro Gold Dagger and [<cite>Spook Street</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2190673) winning the CWA Ian Fleming Steel Dagger. The sixth title in the series, [<cite>Joe Country</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2562130), is published on 11 June.

You can find out more about Mick in our [Meet the Author interview](/new-suggestions/meet-the-author/meet-the-author-mick-herron/).
