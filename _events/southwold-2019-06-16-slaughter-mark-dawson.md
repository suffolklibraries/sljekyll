Mark Dawson will be talking about his bestselling John Milton, Beatrix Rose, Isabella Rose and Soho Noir series.

<img src="/images/featured/featured-mark-dawson.jpg" alt="Mark Dawson" class="custom-br-50 mw-40 {% include /c/img-float-right.html %}" />

Mark was born in Lowestoft and worked as a DJ, door-to-door ice-cream seller, factory hand, club promoter and lawyer before becoming an extremely successful self-published crime writer, with nearly two million copies of his books sold around the world. He now lives in Wiltshire with his wife and two young children.
