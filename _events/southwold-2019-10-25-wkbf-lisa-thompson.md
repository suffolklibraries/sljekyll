One day, while looking through a mysterious cabinet of curiosities, Maxwell finds himself erased from his life, as if he's never been born. Find out more about Maxwell's story, then make a dog coaster.

<img src="/images/featured/featured-lisa-thompson.jpg" alt="Lisa Thompson" class="custom-br-50 mw-40 {% include /c/img-float-right.html %}" />

Lisa lives in Suffolk with her family. As well as [<cite>The Day I Was Erased</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2494489), she is the author of [<cite>The Light Jar</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2306654) and [<cite>The Goldfish Boy</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2081507), which was nominated for the Carnegie Medal, the Branford Boase Award and the Waterstones Children's Book Prize, and was also a Waterstones Children's Book of the Month.
