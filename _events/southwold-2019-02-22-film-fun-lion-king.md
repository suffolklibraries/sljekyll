Children are invited to watch <cite>The Lion King</cite> (1994) and make their own lion mane, hanging monkey, pop-up meerkat or squeaky trunked elephant whilst enjoying some slimy yet satisfying worms, lion cupcakes, lion cheeses and edible warthog poo!

Under-8s must be accompanied by an adult.
