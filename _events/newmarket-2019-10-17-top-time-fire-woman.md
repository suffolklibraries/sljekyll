Jo Reynolds became the first female firefighter in Britain in 1982. In her autobiography, [<cite>Fire Woman</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2138464), she recounts her experiences of taking on a man's world and winning, dealing with incidents including lethal forest fires, escaped zoo animals and unexploded bombs alonf the way. Come along, find out more, and ask Jo your questions.
