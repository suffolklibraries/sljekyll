Watch <cite>Chitty Chitty Bang Bang</cite> (1968) with arts and crafts, games and snacks.

<img src="/images/featured/featured-chitty-chitty-bang-bang.jpg" alt="Chitty Chitty Bang Bang" class="custom-br-50 mw-40 {% include /c/img-float-right.html %}" />

Make your own flying car, invent (and eat) your own sweets and create some pipe cleaner loolipops or a wacky woolly wig while tooting on a toot sweet.

Children under 8 must be accompanied by an adult. Refreshments available.
