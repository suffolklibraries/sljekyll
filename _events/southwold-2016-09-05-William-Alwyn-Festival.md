The 6th William Alwyn Festival runs from 5th-9th October. Come and buy your
tickets from Southwold Library.

Events include performances at Snape, Orford and Blythburgh by the BBC Concert
Orchaestra, the Odysseus Trio, the Tippett String Quartet and the Prometheus
Orchaestra, as well as film scores by the Electric Picture Palace, Southwold.
Visit: http://wwww.williamalywn.co.uk/ for more details.
