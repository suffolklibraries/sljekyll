Hear about Gaspard the Fox's latest escapades, then make fox masks, plates and puppets.

<img src="/images/featured/featured-james-mayhew-zeb-soanes.jpg" alt="James Mayhew and Zeb Soanes" class="custom-br-50 mw-40 {% include /c/img-float-right.html %}" />

Zeb Soanes is known to millions as a newsreader on BBC Radio 4. His first children's book with James Mayhew, [<cite>Gaspard the Fox</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2396720), was inspired by a real life little fox who visits him in London. The fancy-dress dog show in the sequel, [<cite>Gaspard Best in Show</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2604204), takes place in his local square each autumn.

James Mayhew is an award-winning author and illustrator who lives in Suffolk. His many titles include the bestselling Katie and Ella Bella books. He has been writing and illustrating for nearly 30 years.
