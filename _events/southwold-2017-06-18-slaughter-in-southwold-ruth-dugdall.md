**Ruth Dugdall worked as a probation officer in Suffolk for almost a decade. Ruth's writing is heavily influenced by her professional background, providing authenticity and credibility to the crime genre.**

<img src="/images/featured/featured-ruth-dugdall.jpg" alt="Ruth Dugdall" class="custom-br-50 mw-40 {% include /c/img-float-right.html %}" />

<cite>My Sister and Other Liars</cite> is her sixth novel. A domestic noir, from the perspective of a teenage anorexic, it was released in May 2016 and is set in Ipswich and Felixstowe.

It is the story of two sisters and the secrets and lies at the heart of their world. Sam is 17, starving herself and longing for oblivion; Jena is 30, left brain-damaged and desperate to remember.

Piecing Jena's fragments words together, Sam resolves to find her sister's attacker. At any cost. But the journey of discovery also means a loss of innocence for Sam, with devastating consequences.
