Drop in for the first ever [Takeover Festival](/bloc/events/takeover-festival/), led by young people and celebrating youth voice and creativity in Suffolk. Expect poetry, dance and film workshops, an exhibition showcasing young Suffolk artists' work and live music from:

* Roma - singing
* Sienna Potter - singing and guitar
* Charlie - ukulele
* Ella the Poet - poetry
* Carla - singing and guitar
* Sammy - rapping
* Ollie Bowman - singing and guitar
* Zoe - singing and guitar

There will also be a drop-in workshop from the Young Artists in Suffolk collective, where they will be facilitating discussions around young artists and their needs.

Join us again in the evening for [live music and a licensed bar](/events/ipswich-2019-11-09-takeover-festival-evening/).
