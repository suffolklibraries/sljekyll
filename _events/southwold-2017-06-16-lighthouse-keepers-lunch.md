A new musical storytelling extravaganza to celebrate 40 years of Rondo and David Armitage's <cite>The Lighthouse Keeper's Lunch</cite> packed with live music, audience participation and all the sights and sounds of the sea.

The production will be followed by more lighthouse keeper stories and lighthouse crafts with Southwold Library at 5pm at Southwold Arts Centre.

Book your tickets by calling Southwold Arts Centre on [01502 722572](tel:01502722572).
