Congratulations to all of our young readers who completed The Summer Reading
Challenge, we will be celebrating your achievement in our Medals and Certificates
Presentation Ceremony. Bring along your family and friends, we hope to see you
there! There will also be puppet shows at 10:30 am, and another starting at midday.

There will be two presentations starting at 10am and 11:30 am.
The awards will be presented by Town Councillor Neal Beecroft- Smith and Suffolk
Libraries Executive Alison Wheeler.
