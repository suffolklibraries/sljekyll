Join us for a screening of <cite>Yesterday</cite>. Refreshments, bar and raffle available.

> "Directed by Danny Boyle and written by Richard Curtis, this romantic comedy stars Himesh Patel as Jack Malik a struggling musician in a small seaside town. After an accident Jack finds he is the only person who remembers the Beatles and his path to fame and fortune opens wide. Pure fantasy of joyous proportions and a delight for all Beatles fans. It also features Ed Sheeran and was shot on location in Suffolk! "
