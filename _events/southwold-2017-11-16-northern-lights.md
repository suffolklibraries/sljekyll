To mark the launch of Philip Pullman's [<cite>The Book of Dust vol 1: La Belle Sauvage</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2160757), we're holding a special book discussion of [<cite>Northern Lights</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=1726172). You don't have to be a member of a book club - just come along and join the conversation.

If you want to read or reread <cite>Northern Lights</cite>, copies are available to buy from Southwold Books or reserve from the library. eBook and eAudiobook versions are also available to borrow on [OverDrive](https://suffolklibraries.overdrive.com/search?query=northern+lights) and [BorrowBox](https://fe.bolindadigital.com/wldcs_bol_fo/b2i/productDetail.html?productId=BOL_097074&fromPage=1&b2bSite=4172).
