Meet the 'spiky-nosed' characters from <cite>Football School</cite>, <cite>Billy Bonkers</cite>, <cite>Ally's World</cite> and <cite>Max Archer, Kid Detective</cite>, then have a go at creating, drawing and making a world for your own character.

<img src="/images/featured/featured-spike-gerrell.jpg" alt="Spike Gerrell" class="custom-br-50 mw-40 {% include /c/img-float-right.html %}" />

Spike lives in north London. As well as illustrating a wealth of children's books, his work has appeared in the <cite>New Scientist</cite>, <cite>Independent</cite>, <cite>Times Educational Supplement</cite> and the <cite>Guardian</cite>.
