Children are invited to watch <cite>The Smurfs</cite> (2011), make Smurf balloons, masks and toadstool houses, give themselves blue Smurf faces, make and eat an edible toadstool and try a Smurf cupcake and a range of blue Smurf food.

Under 8s must be accompanied by an adult.
