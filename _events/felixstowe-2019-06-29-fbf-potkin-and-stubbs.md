Join us to hear author and Suffolk librarian Sophie Green talk about her debut novel [<cite>Potkin and Stubbs</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2520242), the first in a trilogy about a determined young reporter and her ghostly companion solving mysteries on the streets of Peligan City. Sophie will be interviewed by a panel of young people who have read this book at school.

This event is free but booking is required.
