Award-winning crime writer Elly Griffiths will be at the library talking about her new book, [<cite>The Dark Angel</cite>](https://suffolk.spydus.co.uk/cgi-bin/spydus.exe/ENQ/OPAC/BIBENQ?BRN=2317358), the 10th Dr Ruth Galloway mystery.

Elly will be signing copies of her books, with books available to buy on the day.

Ticket price includes refreshments and nibbles.

Tickets available online and from the ground floor enquiry desk.
