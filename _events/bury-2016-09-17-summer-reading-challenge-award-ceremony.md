Congratulations to all of our young readers who completed The Summer Reading
Challenge, we will be celebrating your achievement in our Medals and Certificates
Presentation Ceremony. Bring along your family and friends, we hope to see you
there!

The winners of the 'Design a Crazy Character' competition and the Treasure Hunt
will also be announced at the event.

Please arrive promptly for the session which contains your surname.

Surnames: A-K: 10am
Surnames: L-Z: 11:30am

Local Councillor and Mayor of Edmundsbury Julia Wakelam will be presenting the
awards.
