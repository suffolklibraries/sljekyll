Congratulations to all of our young readers who completed The Summer Reading
Challenge, we will be celebrating your achievement in our Medals and Certificates
Presentation Ceremony. Bring along your family and friends, we hope to see you
there!

County councillor Julian Flood and the major of Haverhill, Councillor David Roach,
will be presenting the awards.

Haverhill Library's Summer Reading Challenge Presentation will be split into the following time slots:

* Surnames A-F: 10:00-10:45
* Surnames G-L: 11:15-12:00
* Surnames M-R: 14:00-14:45
* Surnames S-Z: 15:00-15:45
